About

A brand started as a hobby, by a teenager. All our products are hand-made with love. Customizing options are also available. We can also preserve your flowers so they can last forever and the good part is you can also wear them as an accessory. Our brand, rooted in the beauty of nature and the art of preservation, is dedicated to craft exquisite resin pendants and beads jewelry that capture the ephemeral charm of flowers. With a deep appreciation for the delicate intricacies of flora, we carefully harvest and preserve these blossoms, encapsulating their grace and vibrancy in our meticulously handcrafted resin creations. Each pendant tells a unique story, encapsulating the emotions and memories associated with the flowers they hold. Our commitment to sustainability extends to our beads jewelry, where we combine these floral treasures with a variety of beads to create elegant, nature-inspired pieces that evoke a sense of timeless beauty. Our brand is a celebration of the natural world, transforming fleeting moments into everlasting pieces of wearable art.
